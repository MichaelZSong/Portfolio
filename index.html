About Me

As a fourth-year SFU SIAT student, I specialize in Media Arts, creating digital media using custom vector graphics. My design philosophy is inspired by modern minimalism, emphasizes clean and functional designs with a meticulous attention to detail.

Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, I use it to bring my design concepts to life. I believe that great designs should be functional and easy to understand. I strive to create designs that serve their intended purpose effectively while also looking visually appealing.

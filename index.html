About Me

As a fourth-year SFU Interactive Arts and Technology student, I specialize in Media Arts, creating digital artworks using custom vector graphics in Adobe Creative Suite. My design philosophy is inspired by modern minimalism, and I am passionate about using clean, elegant typography to complement my graphics and create a harmonious overall aesthetic.

Proficient in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Lightroom, After Effects, and InDesign, I have honed my skills in visual design and attention to detail. I believe that great design should be both beautiful and functional, easy to navigate and understand. I strive to create designs that not only look good but also serve their intended purpose.
